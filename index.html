About Me

Ever since the 6th grade, programming has been my passion. I still remember being in awe at a girl programming in the middle of typing class, seeing her fingers go at mach speed coding whatever came to her head. I see her press the run button and see a fun little game be created. After that moment, I wanted to learn the ability to create whatever came to my mind so I focused my free time and any time I could in school on coding. Throughout middle and high school, I went to creating any ideas in my head for projects in my IDE. I went from printing a simple “Hello World” to my Neural Network learning thousands of handwriting and being able to read any handwriting. I learned Java in a regular AP Computer Science course, but Python and Machine Learning was learned through tearing through any book I could find and watching any video tutorial on the web. My curiosity has yet to peak.

This passion now carried over to my education here at the University of Minnesota. Here, I am now able to access resources that I never knew existed. I began to study and develop coding languages, algorithms, mathematics, and anything that could help me unlock newer and greater paths. Even after learning and knowing as much as I do now, I still haven’t even reached the start of how much there was to learn.

Now, I hope to continue my passion into the Industry of software development and artificial intelligence. I am still only beginning my journey but I am excited and ready to solve new problems and build incredible projects.
